dangli is a brand from Berlin that offers unique, modern handmade jewelry. Each piece is meticulously crafted by laser-cutting mostly leftover wood and hand-painted by me. The collection includes a range of designs, from long dangly earrings—hence the name “dangli“—to studs and necklaces. My designs are predominantly geometric and occasionally draw inspiration from nature. They all originate from digital vector shapes I create or from my drawings that I digitize. Additionally, I enjoy reassembling jewelry I discover at flea markets or in my own drawers into chic new pieces.

dangli stands for modern, unique designs and offers a stylish hand-made alternative to mass-produced jewelry. It is created using the innovative technology of laser cutting and the sustainable resource of wood. Emphasizing sustainability, dangli strives to be conscious of the planet.
